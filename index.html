Legacy of Excellence

Fostering Global Diplomacy

Since our inception, New Model United Nations has stood as a premier academic forum fostering institutional diplomacy, rigorous multilateral negotiation, and high-stakes strategic compromise. Bringing together passionate global delegates from diverse fields, we cultivate critical leadership frameworks, public advocacy proficiency, and international legal literacy. Here, the complex paradigms of global politics meet structural innovation, empowering the next generation of leaders to draft enduring solutions for the world's most critical sovereign, economic, and security milestones.

From the Desk of the Principal

"Dear Delegates, Faculty and Readers, It is with immense admiration that I extend my warmest greetings to all participants of this year’s (2025) School Model United Nations. Watching our student’s step into the roles of diplomats, negotiators, and changemakers fills me with hope for the future. MUN is more than an academic exercise—it is a powerful platform where young minds engage with global challenges, practice empathy, and learn the art of respectful dialogue. In a world that often feels divided, your commitment to collaboration and understanding is a beacon of possibility. To every delegate who research tirelessly, debate passionately, and listen deeply: you are already shaping the world. May this experience ignite a lifelong passion for leadership, justice, and peace. Thanks"

Mr. Kollath Gopinath Menon

Principal of The New Indian School
